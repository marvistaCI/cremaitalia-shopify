Our model

Curated, never aggregated.

We are not a roaster. We do not roast, grind, blend, or repackage. We import whole-bean coffee in the valve-sealed bags our roasters fill at origin, and we ship them to you unchanged.

We choose a small number of roasters whose work represents the craft of Italian roasting at its best, and we hold the U.S. consumer channel for each of them. Fewer roasters, deeper relationships, honest pricing.

Everything is whole bean — because that is what our roasters seal at origin. We do not sell ground coffee.

The Opportunity · One-time

Offerta

Limited-time offers, on sale while inventory lasts.

Coffee lots that have reached their Offerta threshold — still good, still within a real freshness window, priced down honestly. We show the original price struck through, the Offerta price beside it, and the actual remaining freshness window in plain words. No fake urgency, no countdown timers.

Sold as-is. Offerta purchases carry a modified guarantee — material defects only. Freshness window is disclosed at purchase; no exchanges for age-related palate preferences. Coffee that falls outside its freshness window is removed from sale entirely and donated to Feeding Tampa Bay.
At launch, Offerta is typically empty — coffee only arrives here as a lot ages past its threshold. The example below shows how an Offerta listing reads.

Who we are

About Crema Italia

A Florida importer and curator of specialty Italian coffee. Not a roaster.

Steve
Founder

Crema Italia, LLC imports specialty whole-bean coffee from a small, named group of Italian roasters, stores it in our Florida warehouse, and ships it to American customers unchanged. We are the U.S. Agent for FDA food-facility registration on behalf of our roaster partners.

Curated, never aggregated

We do not roast, grind, blend, or repackage. We choose a small number of roasters whose work represents the craft of Italian roasting at its best, and we present their coffee exactly as they sealed it.

The first sale is an introduction

A Sorpresa Tour is how most people meet us — three roasters in one box, our choice. The second sale is the business: a Roccia subscription with the roaster you fell for.

First-bag satisfaction guarantee

On Roccia, Sorpresa, and Selezione, if your first bag isn't right for you, we'll send a different one — free. We'd rather find your coffee than win an argument.

Offerta is different — sold as-is

Offerta lots are good coffee approaching the edge of their freshness window, priced down honestly. They carry a modified guarantee: material defects only, with the freshness window disclosed at purchase. No exchanges for age-related palate preferences.

Damage replacement

If a bag arrives damaged, tell us and we'll replace it.

Freshness window

Best within 60 days of roast date; for peak flavor, brew within 30. We always show the roast date — never a bare "best by."

Our no-waste pledge

Coffee that passes its freshness window is removed from sale and donated to Feeding Tampa Bay. We don't discount our way out of waste; we give it away.

Questions

FAQ

Do you sell ground coffee?

No. Everything is whole bean, because that is what our roasters seal in valve bags at origin. Grind fresh just before you brew.

Do you roast the coffee?

No. We import and curate; the roasters roast. We never roast, blend, or repackage.

How do subscriber discounts work?

An active Roccia subscription gives 10% off Roccia, Sorpresa, and Selezione (Founding Members — the first 222 subscribers at launch — receive 12% for life). Offerta and Bottega are never discounted. On your very first order, a one-time first-purchase 5% stacks on top — so a new subscriber's first order is 15% off eligible shelves.

Is there a promo code box at checkout?

No. Any discount you've earned applies through a personal link or automatically when you're signed in — there's no code field to hunt for.

How fresh is the coffee?

Best within 60 days of the roast date, which we always show. Sorpresa Tours are assembled on-demand so every bag ships fresh.
